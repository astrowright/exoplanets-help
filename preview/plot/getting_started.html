Saving Plots for Later Use

The plot views are stored in one of your browser's local storage buckets (either as cookies or using the HTM5 local storage functionality). This means that you must use the same browser on the same computer to load saved plot views. It also means that resetting your browser's cookies and/or local storage will destroy your saved plots.

Exporting Plots

Exporting plots can take a few seconds -- your plot should begin to download as soon as it is ready.
